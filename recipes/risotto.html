A Risotto Recipe

Ingredients

  • White wine
  • Low-sodium chicken broth
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Shallots
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese

How To Make Risotto

  1. Preheat the Broth.
  2. Cook the Aromatic Vegetables.
  3. Toast the Orzo.
  4. Add wine.
  5. Add the broth.
  6. Add Butter and Parmesan Cheese.
